User Experience

Coburg Banks

Coburg Banks are a recruitment firm based in the Midlands

I was asked to plan out their current site architecture, and propose a new one for their 2 main personas. This process involved using analytics and recording user sessions to see where the current site was failing and inform decisions about the new one. A content audit was performed, and after that wireframes were produced.
