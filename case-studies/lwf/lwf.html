User Experience, in-browser design

Love Worth Finding

LWF has a multi-million dollar video and audio ministry in the US.

Their original site was overly complicated, incredibly hard to navigate and inconsistent throughout. I restructured the site to enable a more fluid user journey, and produced a much cleaner UI.

The design process, by client request, needed to be quick, so I went from site architecture, straight to in-browser design. Having fast feedback from the client was critical.
