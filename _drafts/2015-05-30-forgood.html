Here I'll say it; I'm fed up designing apps or websites for the next tinder; the next instagram. I want to do work that either helps people in need, or moves the human race forwards. I need to feel like I'm contributing to the greater good, and not adding to the noise that distracts us from what's really important; people, relationships and what we do with our lives.

Introducing forgood.io

forgood.io is a freelance collective which only works with organisations that correlate with this manifesto.

  • We will design and build your next app if helps those in poverty.
  • We will make your website if it highlights the plight of child trafficking.
  • We will not make your next app if it adds filters to photos.
  • We will not make your website if it encourages people to buy more pairs of socks.

Get the idea?
